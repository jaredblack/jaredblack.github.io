Trivia Night

Rules

  • We will divide randomly into 3 teams
  • Each team will have one representative at the front of the room answering questions at any time
  • Every 3 questions, all 3 team representatives swap out with someone else from their team
  • The representative from whichever team answered the last question correctly gets to select the next question.
  • Team representatives answer in the order that the buzzers were pressed until someone gets it right. If nobody gets it right, the general team gets a chance to answer in buzzer order for half points.
  • Please don’t answer any questions if it’s not your turn to do so!
  • Be kind - some of the questions are hard and it’s hard to be put on the spot. The point is to have fun

Answer of the Night

For each Trivia Night event, there will be an "answer of the night" hidden somewhere. The first person to answer any question with the answer of the night will win their team 1000 points and a chance for anyone on their team to answer current question for full points. Tonight's answer of the night is found by solving the riddle: I am an odd number. Take away a letter and I become even. What number am I?
