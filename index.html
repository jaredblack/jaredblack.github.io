Answer of the Night

For each Trivia Night event, there will be an "answer of the night" hidden somewhere. The first person to answer any question with the answer of the night will win their team 1000 points and a chance for anyone on their team to answer current question for full points. Tonight's answer of the night is found by solving the riddle: I am an odd number. Take away a letter and I become even. What number am I?
